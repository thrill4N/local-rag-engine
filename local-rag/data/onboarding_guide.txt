New Employee Onboarding Guide

Section 1: IT Support and Accounts
All IT support requests should be directed to helpdesk@examplecorp.internal
or filed as a ticket through the IT Service Portal, not sent directly to
individual IT staff members. The portal is the only channel with a
guaranteed response-time SLA: 4 business hours for standard requests, and
30 minutes for anything tagged "urgent — access blocked." New hires
receive their primary email and single sign-on (SSO) credentials from IT
on their first morning, provisioned automatically the night before their
start date based on the offer letter's start date field.

Section 2: Equipment Provisioning
Standard equipment (laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse) is shipped to the
employee's home address 5 business days before their start date for remote
hires, or waiting at their desk on day one for office-based hires.
Employees needing non-standard equipment (a second monitor, specialized
peripherals, accessibility equipment) should request it through their
manager during the first week; these requests typically take 7-10
business days to fulfill.

Section 3: First Week Schedule
Day one consists of a company-wide orientation session (9am-12pm) followed
by IT setup and account verification in the afternoon. Days two and three
are reserved for department-specific onboarding led by the new hire's
manager. By the end of week one, every new employee should have completed
the compliance training module in the Learning Portal — this is mandatory
and tracked; HR follows up directly with the employee's manager if it is
not completed within 5 business days of the start date.

Section 4: Benefits Enrollment
New employees have 30 days from their start date to enroll in health,
dental, and vision benefits through the Benefits Portal. Enrollment
outside this 30-day window is only possible during the annual open
enrollment period in November, except in the case of a qualifying life
event (marriage, birth of a child, loss of other coverage), which reopens
a 30-day enrollment window from the date of the event.
